Cookie Policy

This policy explains what cookies CulinEire sets, why we set them, and how you can manage them. CulinEire is operated by Bearcave Limited (Company No. 658124), registered in Ireland.

What is a cookie?

About Cookies

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. Cookies help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and protect forms from abuse. They do not contain executable code and cannot access other files on your device.

Essential cookies

Cookies We Set

CulinEire sets only essential, functional cookies. We do not set advertising, tracking, or profiling cookies, and we do not share cookie data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookie name Purpose Duration Legal basis
sessionid Maintains your login session so you stay signed in as you navigate the site. Session (cleared on browser close) Strictly necessary
csrftoken Protects forms against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks. 1 year Strictly necessary
ce_pwa_* Remembers whether you have dismissed the app install prompt so it is not shown repeatedly. 7 days Strictly necessary (localStorage)

Note: ce_pwa_* values are stored in your browser's localStorage, not as HTTP cookies. They serve the same functional purpose and are subject to the same controls.

Third parties

Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party services used by CulinEire may set their own cookies:

  • Cloudflare Turnstile (privacy-preserving CAPTCHA used on forms) may set cookies to verify you are human. Turnstile is designed to minimise data collection. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts is loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect limited technical information such as IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

CulinEire does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other behavioural tracking or advertising technology.

Your rights

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. The following links explain how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies (such as sessionid and csrftoken) will prevent you from logging in or using interactive features on CulinEire.
