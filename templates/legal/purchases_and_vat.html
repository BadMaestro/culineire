How Purchases Work

CulinEire is operated by Bearcave Limited (Company No. 658124, VAT IE3645402WH), registered in Ireland. All paid features on the platform are processed securely through Stripe. This page explains what you can buy, how VAT is applied, and the rules that govern purchased items.

Operator Bearcave Limited
VAT number IE3645402WH
Payment processor Stripe
VAT rate 23% (Ireland)
Currency Euro (€)

VAT

  • All prices displayed on CulinEire are quoted exclusive of VAT.
  • VAT at the applicable rate is calculated automatically by Stripe and added at checkout before payment is taken.
  • The applicable rate for customers in Ireland is 23%.
  • Bearcave Limited is VAT-registered in Ireland: VAT number IE3645402WH.
  • Where VAT does not apply (for example, for customers in certain jurisdictions), Stripe will determine this at checkout based on your location.

Arena Tokens

Arena Tokens are the virtual currency of the CulinEire Chef Battle Arena. They are purchased in packages and credited to your token wallet instantly after a successful payment. Tokens operate as a closed-loop digital currency and have no monetary value outside the arena.

  • Token package prices shown in the Token Shop are shown exclusive of VAT. The final price including 23% Irish VAT is displayed at Stripe checkout before payment is taken.
  • Tokens are credited to your account wallet instantly after payment is confirmed by Stripe.
  • Tokens have no cash value. They cannot be withdrawn, transferred to another account, sold, exchanged for cash, or redeemed for any goods or services outside the arena.
  • Tokens are non-refundable once credited to your wallet, except where required by Irish consumer protection law or other applicable law.
  • Tokens can only be spent within the arena: sending appreciation gifts, purchasing combat artifacts, and sending battle gifts to chefs during active battles.
  • Unused tokens remain in your wallet while your account is active. Balances on accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be expired at CulinEire's discretion.
  • All token transactions are recorded in an immutable audit ledger. Once recorded, a transaction cannot be reversed except under the refund rules above.
  • You must be 18 years of age or older to purchase tokens or send gifts in the arena.

CBR & LSR Rewards

CulinEire may issue discretionary token grants to chefs and live supporters as recognition of activity in the arena.

  • Chef Battle Rewards (CBR) are token grants issued to chefs who win battles, reach level milestones, or are recognised for outstanding performance.
  • Live Support Rewards (LSR) are token grants issued to viewers who send qualifying appreciation gifts during live battles.
  • CBR and LSR are issued at CulinEire's sole discretion and are not guaranteed. They do not constitute a contractual entitlement.
  • Reward tokens are subject to the same closed-loop restrictions as purchased tokens. They cannot be withdrawn or exchanged for cash.
  • Reward amounts and eligibility criteria may change at any time without notice.

Digital Items (Gifts & Artifacts)

  • Appreciation gifts and combat artifacts are digital items only. No physical goods are supplied.
  • Gifts are displayed on the recipient chef's profile as digital recognition. They have no monetary value.
  • Artifacts are consumed or displayed within the arena. They cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed outside the platform.
  • Chef's Battle is a skill-based cooking competition. Purchasing tokens or sending gifts does not constitute placing a bet and does not entitle the sender to any prize or monetary return.

Operator-Issued Artifacts

The Arena Operator may, at their sole discretion, grant artifacts directly to chefs as recognition awards, competition prizes, or promotional items.

  • Operator-issued artifacts are granted for in-arena use only. They are not a purchase and carry no monetary value under any circumstances.
  • Operator-issued artifacts cannot be converted into tokens, redeemed for cash, transferred to another user, or withdrawn from the platform in any form.
  • Attempting to sell, trade, or monetise an operator-issued artifact outside the arena constitutes a breach of these terms and may result in immediate account suspension.
  • Operator-issued artifacts are governed exclusively by the Arena Rules and these terms. No third-party rights arise from their issuance.
  • The Arena Operator reserves the right to revoke operator-issued artifacts at any time without notice or compensation.

Sponsorships

CulinEire offers paid sponsor placements on the Sponsor Puzzle. Prices are quoted exclusive of VAT. Full terms, placement rules, and refund conditions are set out in the Sponsor Agreement.

  • Sponsorship payments are processed through Stripe Checkout.
  • Payment reserves a sponsor slot for compliance review and staff approval. It does not guarantee publication or activation.
  • A VAT invoice is issued by Bearcave Limited and sent to the sponsor's email address upon activation.

Payment Processing

  • All payments are handled by Stripe. CulinEire does not store card details.
  • Stripe may apply additional verification steps at checkout in accordance with Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) requirements under PSD2.
  • Payments are denominated in Euro (€).

Refunds

  • Token purchases are non-refundable once tokens have been credited to your wallet, except where required by Irish consumer protection law.
  • Sponsorship refund conditions are described in the Sponsor Agreement.
  • If you believe a charge was made in error, contact us at the contact page.

Contact

For billing queries, VAT invoices, or any question about a payment, please use the contact form. Include your order reference or the email address used at checkout.
