Refunds & Chargebacks

  • Token purchases are non-refundable once credited to your wallet, except where required by Irish consumer protection law or confirmed as a technical or billing error by CulinEire.
  • By purchasing tokens and using them in the arena, you waive your EU right of withdrawal for that digital content, as confirmed at checkout.
  • If you believe a charge was made in error, contact us via the contact page before initiating a chargeback with your card issuer.
  • Chargeback consequences: if a payment is reversed or charged back, CulinEire will review all token credits and rewards associated with that purchase. Related token balances may be reduced, pending reward records may be reversed or voided, and the account may be placed under compliance review. CulinEire reserves the right to suspend arena access pending resolution.
  • Financial records (token transactions, reward records, ledger events) are never silently deleted. All adjustments are recorded in the immutable audit ledger with a reason.
  • Sponsorship refund conditions are in the Sponsor Agreement.

Arena Tokens

Arena Tokens are the virtual currency of the CulinEire Chef Battles Arena. They operate as a closed-loop digital currency and have no monetary value outside the arena.

  • Tokens are credited to your wallet instantly after payment is confirmed by Stripe.
  • Tokens have no cash value. They cannot be withdrawn, transferred, sold, exchanged for cash, or redeemed for any goods or services outside the arena.
  • Tokens are non-refundable once credited to your wallet, except where required by applicable law.
  • Tokens can only be spent within the arena: appreciation gifts, combat artifacts, and battle gifts to chefs.
  • Unused tokens remain in your wallet while your account is active. Balances on accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be expired at CulinEire's discretion.
  • All token transactions are recorded in an immutable audit ledger and cannot be reversed except under the refund rules above.
  • You must be 18 years of age or older to purchase tokens or send gifts in the arena.

CBR & LSR Rewards

CulinEire may issue discretionary token grants to Chefs and live supporters as recognition of activity in the arena.

  • Chef Battles Rewards (CBR) are token grants issued to Chefs who win battles, reach level milestones, or are recognised for outstanding performance.
  • Live Support Rewards (LSR) are token grants issued to viewers who send qualifying appreciation gifts during live battles.
  • CBR and LSR are issued at CulinEire's sole discretion and are not guaranteed. They do not constitute a contractual entitlement.
  • Reward tokens are subject to the same closed-loop restrictions as purchased tokens. They cannot be withdrawn or exchanged for cash.
  • Reward amounts and eligibility criteria may change at any time without notice.

Digital Items (Gifts & Artifacts)

  • Appreciation gifts and combat artifacts are digital items only. No physical goods are supplied.
  • Gifts are displayed on the recipient Chef's profile as digital recognition. They have no monetary value.
  • Artifacts are consumed or displayed within the arena. They cannot be transferred, sold, or redeemed outside the platform.
  • Chef Battles is a skill-based cooking competition. Purchasing tokens or sending gifts does not constitute placing a bet and does not entitle the sender to any prize or monetary return.

Operator-Issued Artifacts

The Arena Operator may, at their sole discretion, grant artifacts directly to Chefs as recognition awards, competition prizes, or promotional items.

  • Operator-issued artifacts are granted for in-arena use only. They are not a purchase and carry no monetary value under any circumstances.
  • Operator-issued artifacts cannot be converted into tokens, redeemed for cash, transferred to another user, or withdrawn from the platform in any form.
  • Attempting to sell, trade, or monetise an operator-issued artifact outside the arena constitutes a breach of these terms and may result in immediate account suspension.
  • Operator-issued artifacts are governed exclusively by the Arena Rules and these terms. No third-party rights arise from their issuance.
  • The Arena Operator reserves the right to revoke operator-issued artifacts at any time without notice or compensation.

Sponsorships

CulinEire offers paid sponsor placements on the Sponsor Puzzle. Prices are quoted exclusive of VAT. Full terms are set out in the Sponsor Agreement.

  • Sponsorship payments are processed through Stripe Checkout.
  • Payment reserves a slot for compliance review and staff approval. It does not guarantee publication.
  • A VAT invoice is issued by Bearcave Limited upon activation.

Contact

For billing queries, VAT invoices, or any question about a payment, please use the contact form. Include your order reference or the email address used at checkout. You may also write to Bearcave Limited, 2 The Fairways, Tir Cluain, Midleton, Co. Cork, P25 W8W3, Ireland.
