Before you submit

What you can report

  • Copyright infringement or copied recipe text.
  • Watermarked, stolen, or unlicensed images.
  • Privacy, impersonation, defamation, food safety, spam, or abuse concerns.

Useful evidence

  • The CulinEire page URL you are reporting.
  • The original source URL or permission reference if available.
  • A clear explanation of what appears to be wrong.

If you are a rights holder submitting a formal takedown request, include your name, the original source of the work, and the URL of the content you believe is infringing.
