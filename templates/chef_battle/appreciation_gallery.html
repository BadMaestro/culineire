How gifts work

  • Visit any active battle and open the Gift Panel.
  • Select a Chef and choose a gift. Tokens are deducted immediately.
  • The gift appears live in the battle feed for everyone watching.
  • The receiving Chef earns a Live Support Reward (LSR) equivalent to the gift value.
  • Gifts are only available during active battles (not after completion).

18+ required. Sending appreciation gifts requires age verification. Alcohol-themed gifts (Beer Toast, Whiskey Toast, Cocktail, Champagne) are virtual entertainment items only. No physical alcohol or goods are supplied. Gifts are non-refundable digital items. See Chef Battles Rules for full terms.
