Become a Chef

Chef Battles is a competitive cooking arena for CulinEire authors. Once enrolled, your chef profile is created and you can issue challenges, enter battles, and compete for rankings.

Before you join, note:

  • You must be 18 years of age or older to participate.
  • Battle entries must use your own original recipes and real photos. AI-generated images are not permitted.
  • Tokens purchased are non-refundable digital items. They have no cash value and cannot be withdrawn.
  • Battle results and rewards are subject to the Chef Battle Rules.
  • Your profile, token balance and reward records are personal and non-transferable. Account sharing is not permitted.
  • Bearcave Limited may suspend accounts that violate the rules.

By enrolling you accept the Chef Battle Rules in full. Age confirmation and enrolment timestamp are recorded with your IP address for audit and compliance purposes under Irish and EU consumer protection law.
