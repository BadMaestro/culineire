⚔️ Challenge

Issue a formal challenge to another CulinEire chef. They accept or decline.

Both Chefs upload cooking photos or a video before the deadline. Entries are hidden until both are in.

The community votes. Most votes wins. The champion earns rating, rank, and a shot at the Crown.
