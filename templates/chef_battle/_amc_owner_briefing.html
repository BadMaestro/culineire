3 · Console manual — step by step
  1. Open /chef-battle/master/ while logged in as greenbear. Nobody else can see it (404). Data refreshes every 20 seconds automatically.
  2. Top row: battle status card with a live countdown (shows PAUSED when stopped), both chef cards, the real arena ring (click any chef for their profile), the Audience card (real viewer counts, votes, gifts, crown holder).
  3. Phase rail: the highlighted step is the battle's current stage (1 Challenge → 7 Crown).
  4. Panel 1 — your controls (each asks for confirmation and is written to the audit log):
    • Start Phase — advance the battle to its expected next stage.
    • Lock Ingredients / Open Vote — jump to menu_locked / voting directly.
    • Broadcast Notice — public announcement into the battle feed.
    • Emergency Stop — requires a reason; battle → PAUSED, timers freeze, live streams terminate, both chefs get a message + email. Resume restores the previous stage and shifts all deadlines by the pause duration. End Battle cancels permanently (reason required).
    • Award Crown stays disabled forever — the audience decides.
  5. Panel 4 — moderation: Approve/Flag buttons appear next to entries once cooked photos are submitted; Review/Dismiss on DSA reports; End Stream on live sessions. Adverse actions require a reason and notify the chef.
  6. Panel 7 — money decisions: Approve/Reject on payout requests (Approve triggers the real Stripe transfer — the dialog warns you). Moderators can only file battle reports; every report lands in your inbox.
  7. Footer: poll health, server time, flag state. If it says "Poll failed" the page keeps the last known state — reload.
  8. If something goes wrong: every action you or an operator ever took is in the battle event log (panel 2 shows the latest 20, including private audit entries). Rollback/incident procedures: docs/chef_battle/arena_master_console/P09_ROLLOUT_ROLLBACK.yaml.
4 · Running a test battle — full walkthrough

Use two test chef accounts (never real user data). CHEF_BATTLE_ENABLED stays off for the public — as a superuser you see everything anyway.

  1. Prepare two chefs: both need author profiles, arena enrolment (Enter Arena → enrol) and at least 5 energy (publishing a recipe gives +5; or grant moves via the moderation tools).
  2. Issue the challenge: log in as chef A → this page → New Challenge → pick chef B, theme, type. Chef B (or you, logged in as B) accepts it here under "Received".
  3. Acceptance creates the battle (status: scheduled). It appears immediately in the console — battle ID in the top bar, chefs in the cards, facing pair in the ring centre.
  4. Drive it from the console: Start Phase walks it through menu_locked → active (combat). In combat, chefs declare attack/defend from the battle room; panel 3 shows declarations and hits live.
  5. Cooking & moderation: after combat/biathlon, chefs upload cooked photos in the battle room; approve both in panel 4 — the battle advances to presentation.
  6. Voting: Open Vote (or Start Phase). Vote from spectator accounts; panel 5 shows totals, percentages and rejected duplicate attempts in real time.
  7. Completion: Start Phase from voting runs the real result service — winner, rating, crown, rewards. The win celebration fires on the public site.
  8. Try the safety net: hit Emergency Stop mid-battle, watch the timer freeze, then Resume — deadlines shift so no battle time is lost.
  9. Clean up: End Battle (cancel) on anything you don't want to keep; cancelled battles never touch ratings.
