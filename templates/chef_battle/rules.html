Chef Battle Arena

Rules of the Arena

How Chef Battle works, from challenge to crown.

{# ── 1. The Challenge ── #}

1. The Challenge

  • Any chef with an active profile can challenge another chef.
  • Challenges can only be sent between chefs within one level of each other. A Level 1 chef cannot challenge a Level 3 chef.
  • The challenged chef has 48 hours to accept or refuse.
  • Refusing a challenge costs 2 Battle Moves. Repeated refusals are recorded on the public profile.
  • An accepted challenge opens a Battle Room with a shared theme set by the challenger.
{# ── 2. The Battle ── #}

2. The Battle

  • Each chef has 5 days to submit a recipe as their battle entry.
  • Entries must be original recipes created specifically for the battle theme.
  • Only one entry per chef per battle is allowed.
  • After both entries are submitted, the Combat Phase begins.
{# ── 3. Combat Phase ── #}

3. Combat Phase

  • Each chef selects a menu of ingredients from their recipe. Both menus are equal in size.
  • Each chef locks 2 ingredients to protect them from attack.
  • Chefs exchange hits targeting unlocked ingredients in the opponent's menu.
  • A hit ingredient is marked as defeated and must be replaced or removed from the final recipe.
{# ── 4. Biathlon ── #}

4. Biathlon

  • The chef who submitted their recipe first fires up to 3 blind shots at the opponent's ingredient list.
  • A shot that lands on a locked ingredient bounces back and counts as a wasted shot.
  • Results: 1 to 3 successful hits depending on aim and opponent's locks.
  • Killed ingredients must be swapped or removed before cooking begins.
{# ── 5. Cooking Phase ── #}

5. Cooking Phase

  • After combat, both chefs cook their final recipes using the surviving ingredient list.
  • Each chef photographs the finished dish and uploads it to the battle room.
  • A CulinEire moderator reviews the photo to confirm the dish is real, follows the rules, and image rights are clear.
  • Once both entries are approved, the battle moves to public voting.
{# ── 6. Voting ── #}

6. Voting

  • The public votes for their favourite dish. Voting is open for 2 days.
  • Each visitor gets one vote per battle. No account required to vote.
  • The chef with the most votes wins the battle and receives the Crown.
  • In the event of a tie, the battle is recorded as a draw and no crown is awarded.
  • The Crown is held for 24 hours and is visible on the winner's public profile.
{# ── 7. Ranking ── #}

7. Ranking and Levels

  • Winning a battle earns +25 rating points. Losing costs 15 rating points (minimum 0).
  • Chefs progress through 5 levels. Each level requires 3 wins. Level 5 chefs with 15 or more wins can reach CulinEire Hero status.
  • Ranks are calculated from rating: Commis, Chef de Partie, Sous Chef, Head Chef, Executive Chef, Michelin Chef.
  • A win streak bonus tracks consecutive wins on the public profile.
{# ── 8. Battle Moves ── #}

8. Battle Moves

  • Battle Moves are earned by winning battles, having recipes and articles approved, and reaching milestones.
  • A minimum of 10 Battle Moves is required to issue a challenge.
  • New chefs receive starting moves to enter the arena.
{# ── 9. Tokens ── #}

9. Tokens

  • Tokens (T) are the arena currency. They are purchased in packages or earned through drops.
  • Tokens are used to send appreciation gifts to chefs and to purchase combat artifacts.
  • Tokens have no cash value and cannot be withdrawn.

{# ── 11. Artifact Drops ── #}

11. Artifact Drops

After every completed battle, chefs receive a random artifact from the arena vault.

  • The winner always receives a drop.
  • The loser has a 50% chance of receiving a consolation drop.
  • Artifacts cannot be duplicated. If all artifacts of a given rarity are already owned, the system draws from another rarity.
  • Artifacts are displayed permanently on the chef's profile.

{# ── 12. Artifacts ── #}

12. Combat Artifacts

  • There are 200 combat artifacts: 100 attack and 100 defence, across 5 rarities.
  • Artifacts can also be purchased in the arena shop using tokens.
  • Rarity determines token cost: Common 10T, Uncommon 25T, Rare 60T, Epic 150T, Legendary 400T.
  • Artifacts affect combat mechanics. Attack artifacts target opponent ingredients. Defence artifacts protect your own.
