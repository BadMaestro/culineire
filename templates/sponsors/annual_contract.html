Placement Options

CulinEire offers three sponsorship placements on the Sponsor Puzzle, operated by Bearcave Limited (Company No. 658124, VAT IE3645402WH). All prices are quoted exclusive of VAT. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.

Weekly Ring Sponsor

A 7-day ring cell placement. One-off payment, no auto-renewal. Display your logo or avatar on the CulinEire Sponsor Puzzle for one week.

Annual Ring Sponsor

A 12-month ring cell placement. One-off annual payment. Your logo or avatar stays on the Sponsor Puzzle for a full year.

Sponsor of the Month

The central cell. 30-day placement at €1,000 + VAT. Your brand is highlighted across site announcements and social channel notifications alongside every new recipe and article published during your term.
{# ── Weekly Ring detail ────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}
Weekly Ring

7-Day Ring Sponsorship

  • A single ring cell on the CulinEire Sponsor Puzzle for 7 calendar days from the activation date.
  • Price is set per cell and quoted exclusive of VAT. VAT is added at Stripe Checkout.
  • This is a one-off payment for a single 7-day period. It is not a weekly subscription and does not renew automatically.
  • Payment reserves the selected cell for compliance review. It does not guarantee publication.
  • If you wish to sponsor again after the 7-day term, a new application and payment are required.
{# ── Annual Ring detail ────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}
Annual Ring

12-Month Ring Sponsorship

  • A single ring cell on the CulinEire Sponsor Puzzle for 12 months from the activation date.
  • Price is set per cell and quoted exclusive of VAT. VAT is added at Stripe Checkout.
  • This is a one-off annual payment. It is not a recurring subscription and does not renew automatically.
  • Payment reserves the selected cell for compliance review. It does not guarantee publication.
  • When the 12-month term ends, Bearcave Limited may remove or expire the placement and release the cell unless a new written agreement and payment are completed.
{# ── Central Monthly detail ────────────────────────────────────────────── #}
Sponsor of the Month

Central Monthly Sponsorship

  • The central cell on the CulinEire Sponsor Puzzle, reserved exclusively for the Sponsor of the Month.
  • Price: €1,000 per month, exclusive of VAT. VAT is added at Stripe Checkout.
  • The term begins when the approved sponsor image and link are first published and runs for 30 days. It is not a calendar-month or annual product.
  • This is a one-off payment for a single 30-day placement. It is not a recurring Stripe subscription and does not renew automatically.
  • During an active Sponsor of the Month term, new recipes and articles published on CulinEire carry a "Sponsored by: [Sponsor Name]" attribution in site announcements and social channel notifications. This attribution is automatic for the duration of the active term and is removed when the term ends.
  • The Sponsor of the Month is highlighted in the CulinEire Sponsor Puzzle as the central sponsor for the duration of the term.
{# ── Payment ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}
Payment

Pricing, VAT and Payment

  • All prices are quoted exclusive of VAT. VAT at the applicable rate is calculated and added at Stripe Checkout.
  • Payment is processed securely through Stripe. Bearcave Limited does not store card details.
  • Payment reserves the selected sponsor spot for compliance review and staff approval. It does not guarantee publication or activation.
  • Bearcave Limited is VAT-registered in Ireland: VAT number IE3645402WH.
  • CulinEire may accept Irish or international sponsors. Sponsors do not need to be food-related businesses.
{# ── Contract & Invoice ────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}
Documents

Sponsor Agreement and VAT Invoice

  • When a sponsorship is approved and activated, Bearcave Limited sends a Sponsor Agreement PDF and a VAT Invoice PDF to the sponsor's email address.
  • The Sponsor Agreement confirms the placement details, term, payment summary and the governing terms of the sponsorship.
  • The VAT Invoice is a full Irish VAT invoice issued by Bearcave Limited (t/a CulinEire), VAT IE3645402WH, and is suitable for accountant and tax records.
  • The invoice is generated automatically at the time of activation. The supply date on the invoice is the activation date of the sponsorship.
  • If you need a copy of your documents, contact culineire@bearcave.ie.

Approval

  • Bearcave Limited may refuse, delay, cancel, suspend or reject a sponsorship application where legal, payment, compliance, sanctions, fraud, content, reputational, technical or policy concerns arise.
  • A paid sponsor spot is not published automatically. The submitted logo or avatar becomes visible only after Bearcave Limited approves and publishes it.
  • By submitting a sponsor application, the applicant confirms that neither they, nor the company or organisation represented, nor any relevant owner, director or controlling person, is subject to EU, UN, Irish or other applicable financial sanctions.
  • Bearcave Limited cannot accept sponsorship from persons or entities subject to applicable sanctions, asset freezes or restrictive measures.
  • If Bearcave Limited declines a paid placement before publication, the application enters the refund-required workflow and Bearcave Limited will process a refund through Stripe. Once a sponsor image has been published, refunds are not guaranteed unless required by applicable law or agreed in writing.
