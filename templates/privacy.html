Data & Trust

What We Store

CulinEire stores the information needed to run author accounts, publish approved recipes and articles, operate moderation tools, send account email, and protect the site from abuse.

  • Account details such as username, email address, display name and selected default avatar.
  • Author profile content, recipes, articles, messages and moderation history created on the site.
  • Essential session, CSRF and security cookies required for login, forms and site protection.
  • Operational security events such as logins, blocked authors and moderation actions.
Control

Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information from your author account.
  • You can request account or author profile deletion through the contact form.
  • CulinEire does not sell account data or share it for advertising.
  • Some records may be retained where needed for security, abuse prevention or legal obligations.

Questions about privacy can be sent through the contact form.
